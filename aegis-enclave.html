Enclave Model

Centralized regulated workspaces with managed operations around them.

Each Aegis customer receives a logically isolated operational enclave designed to support secure collaboration, controlled access, System Security Plan alignment, and repeatable CMMC-aligned workflows.

The platform emphasizes operational consistency: identity controls, workspace policies, patch and configuration management, monitoring, and support processes that remain active after the initial deployment.
